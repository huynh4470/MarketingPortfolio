Liquid Earth eCommerce Store


Project Details

During my time at Digital Main Street, as an eCommerce advisor, I assisted small business owners in enhancing their digital presence.
I worked with Liquid Earth, a local business that specializes in offering natural, eco-friendly, and healthier alternatives for everyday cleaning tasks. Liquid Earth aimed to boost traffic to their eCommerce store and improve the overall usability of their website.

Team

  • 2 Business Owners
  • 1 Marketing Coop Student
  • 1 eCommerce Advisor



Support provided:

  • Consultations on how to effectively use Google Ads
  • Provided one-on-one support to update their Shopify site, with improvements including:
    • Enhancing site usability by revamping the information architecture
    • Implementing a new theme to align the design with their branding
    • Adjusting alignment issues

Duration

4 months

The Process

1

Initial Consultation

To begin, an initial consultation was scheduled to understand their business goals and how I could assist. During this meeting, I learned that Liquid Earth aimed to boost website traffic through Google Ads. I also identified some of the challenges they were facing with their website, including navigation issues and a lack of brand alignment.

2

Background Research and Website Deep Dive

After gaining an understanding of the business and their goals, research was conducted on Google Ads to determine the most effective way to implement it for their needs. Additionally, a thorough analysis of their Shopify site was performed to identify pain points and offer actionable recommendations.

3

Further Consultations and website implementation

Consultations were held to equip Liquid Earth with the knowledge to effectively implement Google Ads. Additionally, direct support was provided to update their website. The site's information architecture was improved by refining the navigation, and brand alignment was strengthened through the introduction of a new theme. Minor adjustments were also made to ensure the website's cohesiveness
