Freshii's Social Channels

As the Social Media Coordinator for Freshii in Brampton, Richmond Hill, and North York from June 2024 to January 2025, I was responsible for developing and executing the social media strategy.
Below are highlights of my work:

Instagram Contest

I executed an Instagram contest to drive follower growth for Freshii. To maximize engagement, I partnered with influencers, implemented targeted hashtags, cross-promoted the contest across our other social media channels, and shared it in relevant Facebook groups to expand reach and awareness.
