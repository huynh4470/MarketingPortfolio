Who Am I?

My marketing journey began with the CDAP program, a unique opportunity that allowed me to help small business owners grow their digital presence. During this experience at Digital Main Street, conducting social media and website audits and providing consultations on digital marketing strategies to help them grow their online presence.

As I continued to grow my expertise, I took on increasingly creative and strategic roles. At Freshii, I sharpened my social media expertise by managing accounts, creating engaging content, and identifying potential influencers and business partnerships. At Love Caravan, I developed reels to promote events and helped update their website to enhance the user experience. In my most recent role at goConfirm, I was responsible for managing and monitoring online communities on platforms like Facebook and Reddit, while also researching potential partnership opportunities.
