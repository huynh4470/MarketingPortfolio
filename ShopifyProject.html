Graphics for BlueCat Learning's Website

During my internship at BlueCat, I had the opportunity to work on the Learning Team's website, which is used to sell their training courses. The goal of this project was to update the site for better alignment with BlueCat's branding and to improve the clarity of course information to reduce the volume of email inquiries.
In this project, I took on the role of graphic designer while also providing UX advice. My responsibilities included creating icons and images for the website to enhance its visual appeal and functionality.

Course Photos

In Adobe Photoshop, I designed six images for the website that clearly showcases the courses while staying consistent with BlueCat's branding.

Design Considerations

  • Ensure the images matches BlueCat's branding
  • Represent the type of course - Instructor-Led vs E-learning
  • Consistency between all images
  • Represent the material being taught
  • Ensure cultural diversity

Icons

It was debated whether icons or photos should be used for the courses. To explore different options, I created some icons for the instructor-led courses in Adobe Illustrator. Ultimately, we decided to go with photos, as they aligned more closely with BlueCat's brand.



Badges

I also designed badges to represent the different certification levels for the courses. The Integrity product has four certification levels, while Edge and Gateway each have one level.

Design Considerations

  • Contrast levels
  • Visibility of text
  • Consistency between badges
  • Differentiate the different levels and products
